How delivery works

  1. Place your order online using the form above.
  2. You will receive an email and WhatsApp confirmation with your order number.
  3. Your bouquet is hand-prepared on the morning of delivery.
  4. Our driver delivers to the recipient within the chosen time slot.
  5. We follow up with a delivery confirmation message once the flowers have arrived.

Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 10:00 in Dainfern and surrounding areas.
