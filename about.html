Our Story

How a small Dainfern home studio grew into a beloved local flower shop.

Where Blossom Bloom Began

Blossom Bloom was founded in 2024 by Mbali Sithole in Dainfern, South Africa, born out of a love for flowers and a belief that every occasion deserves a touch of beauty. What began as a small home-based venture, creating bouquets for friends and family, quickly grew as word spread about the freshness, creativity, and care behind each arrangement.

Today, Blossom Bloom delivers fresh, hand-picked flowers straight to doorsteps across the community, guided by Mbali's original promise to bring joy, beauty, and meaningful connection to every person who receives a Blossom Bloom arrangement.

Meet Mbali Sithole

Mbali is the heart and hands behind every Blossom Bloom arrangement. With a keen eye for colour and composition, she designs each bouquet as a small piece of art, made to lift someone's day. Every arrangement carries her promise of freshness, creativity, and care.

Our Vision

  • To become the most loved and trusted flower shop in the community.
  • To bring joy and beauty into people's lives through fresh, vibrant flowers.
  • To be known for our creativity, quality, and excellent customer service.
  • To inspire emotions and meaningful connections through thoughtful floral designs.

Our Mission

  • To provide fresh, high-quality flowers at affordable prices.
  • To create unique and beautiful floral arrangements for every occasion.
  • To deliver warm, friendly, and professional customer service.
  • To ensure timely and reliable flower delivery every time.
